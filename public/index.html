The Best Cricketer

Wikipedia says,

Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and captain, widely regarded to be the greatest batsmen of all time.

Also, he is the only player to have scored one hundred international centuries, the first batsman to score a double century in a One Day International, the holder of the record for the number of runs in both ODI and Test cricket, and the only player to complete more than 30,000 runs in international cricket.
